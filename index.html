How to read without being distracted with only two steps

Step 1

Turn off all distractions, make sure that nothing will interfere your reading. Turn off notifactions, and read in a place thats calm and silent.

Step 2

When your mind wanders off, always come back to reading, try making your mind only think about reading and reading only. That's all, mind wandering off is normal, just keep on reading and you'll get used to rarely being distracted.
